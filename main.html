Combat Sambo equipment includes protective equipment that ensures the safety of
fighters during training and competitions.

Go to equipment

1. Protective hood - protects the head and face of the fighter from blows and injuries. The hood must be of sufficient strength and comfortable fit so as not to interfere with visibility and breathing.

2. Chest protection - protects the chest and ribs from impacts. A good chest protector should be light but strong and allow freedom of movement.

3. Belly Guard - Protects the abdomen and lower back from impacts. The belly protector should provide freedom of movement and comfort when worn.

4. Perineum Protection - Protects the male and female crotch from impact and injury. The crotch guard should be light but strong enough to provide safety.

5. Arm and Leg Protection - Protects arms and legs from impact and injury. Arm and leg protection should allow freedom of movement and be strong enough to withstand heavy impacts.

6. Gloves and wrestling boots - used to protect the hands and feet, respectively. Gloves and boots should be comfortable and light, but strong enough to withstand hard impacts.
